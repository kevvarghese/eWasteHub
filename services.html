The "e-Waste Hub" platform offers three core services, ensuring a secure, sustainable, and responsible way to dispose of electronic waste. Users can safely recycle their e-waste while contributing to environmental sustainability. In return, they earn valuable reward points, which can be redeemed on leading retail platforms

Collection & Segregation

Users can drop off their electronic waste at designated collection centers, ensuring responsible and eco-friendly disposal

Reward Points System

Users earn reward points based on the condition of their e-waste, which can be redeemed on leading online retail websites, making recycling both beneficial and rewarding

Recycling & Ecofriendly Disposal

Non-repairable e-waste is processed using environmentally safe recycling methods, minimizing landfill waste and promoting sustainability
