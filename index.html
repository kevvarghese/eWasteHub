"eWaste Hub" is an innovative online platform designed to help individuals responsibly dispose of end-of-life or redundant electronic waste. Instead of offering direct cash compensation, the platform rewards users with 'Reward Points,' which can be converted into vouchers redeemable on popular online marketplaces like Amazon, Flipkart, Uber, and Swiggy. India is the world's third-largest producer of electronic waste

  • 2019-20: 1,014,961 metric tons
  • 2020-21: 1,346,496 metric tons
  • 2021-22: 1,601,155 metric tons
  • 2022-23: 1,751,000 metric tons

Less than one-third of this waste was recycled up until 2022
