Frequently Asked Questions

What is eWaste Hub?

eWaste Hub is an e-waste management platform that helps users responsibly dispose of their electronic waste. In return, users earn reward points based on the condition of their e-waste, which can be redeemed on leading online retail websites.

How does the process work?

Step 1: Register on our website and list the e-waste items you want to dispose of.

Step 2: Drop off your e-waste at one of our designated collection centers in your city.

Step 3: Our team evaluates the condition of your e-waste.

Step 4: You earn reward points based on the condition of your items.

Step 5: Redeem your points on our partner online retail websites.

What types of e-waste can I dispose of?

We accept a wide range of electronic waste, including:

• Mobile phones, laptops, and tablets

• Desktop computers and accessories (keyboards, mice, monitors)

• Batteries and chargers

• Other electronic gadgets

How are reward points calculated?

Reward points are based on the type, condition, and usability of the e-waste. Items in good condition with reusable components earn higher points, while non-functional or heavily damaged items receive fewer points.

What happens to my e-waste after disposal?

Once collected, e-waste is either refurbished, resold, or responsibly recycled following environmental guidelines. Our goal is to minimize landfill waste and maximize material recovery.

How do I redeem my reward points?

Your earned points can be redeemed on leading online retail websites. Log in to your account, check your available points, and choose from our list of partnered platforms to redeem your rewards.

Is there a fee for using this service?

No, our service is free! We aim to encourage responsible e-waste disposal while rewarding users for their contributions to sustainability

Is my personal data safe when I dispose of electronic devices?

Yes! Before recycling, we ensure that all personal data on electronic devices is securely erased following industry standards. However, we recommend that you factory reset your devices before disposal
